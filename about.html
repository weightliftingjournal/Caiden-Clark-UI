About Us

If you're anything like us, you're always trying to do a little better each and every day, but you always seem to have too much on your mind, and you're trying to tackle too many things at once.

Maybe you want to get a little fitter, or even start a new workout habit, but it feels like there's so much to keep track of and remember, and with everything else going on it sometimes feels easier to just drop the habit and not worry about exercise.

That's where Buff comes in.

We help you keep track of your workouts, your routines, as well as make adjustments when you increase your reps or weights, and we can even help you keep track of changes you need to make.

Whether you're an experienced lifter, or looking to make a positive change in your fitness and health, Buff helps you keep track of what you're working on, so you can focus on unlocking your potential.
