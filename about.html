What is Buff?

Buff is a fantastic and innovative way to keep track of workout routines and reps. With our brand new and wonderful site, you can log your workouts with the ease of a few buttons. Just by putting in the workout type, how many reps, and how much time it took to complete it, you can keep an easy to read Journal for your workouts.
